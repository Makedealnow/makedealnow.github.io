About MakeDealNow

Our Mission

MakeDealNow was created to help consumers avoid overpaying by making it easier to compare competing offers from businesses.

Instead of spending hours researching providers, requesting quotes, and negotiating prices, buyers can submit one request and compare multiple options in one place.

Our goal is simple: help consumers save money while helping businesses connect with motivated buyers.

How MakeDealNow Works

Step 1: Submit your request.

Step 2: Compare available offers and providers.

Step 3: Choose the option that best fits your needs and budget.

The process is designed to save time, increase competition, and help consumers make informed decisions.
