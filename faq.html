Frequently Asked Questions

Is MakeDealNow free to use?

Yes. Buyers can submit requests and compare opportunities at no cost.

Am I required to accept an offer?

No. You are free to review offers and decide whether any option meets your needs.

How does MakeDealNow work?

Submit a request, compare responses from businesses, and choose the option that works best for you.

What types of requests can I submit?

Home services, insurance, mortgages, vehicles, real estate, business services, health and fitness, and many other categories.

How do businesses participate?

Businesses can apply through our Business Partner application and may receive opportunities that match their services.

How is my information used?

Information may be used to connect buyers with participating businesses and to respond to submitted requests. For additional details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Does MakeDealNow guarantee offers?

No. Availability of offers depends on participating businesses, market conditions, and the details of your request.

How do I contact MakeDealNow?

Visit our Contact page or email MakeDealNow@outlook.com.

How do I become a Business Partner?

Businesses can apply through the Business Partner application form and provide information about their services, service area, and business category.
